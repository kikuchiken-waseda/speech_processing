畳み込み¶

畳み込み積

\[(f*g)(t) =\int f(\tau)g(t-\tau)d\tau\]

畳み込み和(Conv1d)

\[(f*g)(m) =\sum_{n} f(n)g(m-n)\]

Conv2d

\[(I*K)(i,j) =\sum_{m}\sum_{n} I(i,j)K(i-m,j-n)\]
